# Company & Business Context

## Who We Are
A **service company** (not an OEM) that builds, maintains, and sells the SDV body controller platform to OEM customers.

## The Sell
- Proposals and architecture docs are **sales/consulting deliverables** aimed at OEM senior management
- The "ask" is for the OEM to **buy and adopt** the platform — not an internal budget request
- Language should be accessible and business-focused, not purely technical

## Core Value Proposition
- Decouples body features from Tier-1 body supplier lock-in ← **primary pain point to emphasize**
- OEM gains ownership and auditability of Feature Business Logic
- Body features run autonomously (no head unit / HMI dependency)
- Partnership model: Platform Provider supplies BSW + Linux + hardware; OEM owns application logic

## What to Avoid in Proposals
- Do NOT frame HMI as a top-level benefit
- Do NOT use adversarial "replace the Tier-1" narrative — use partnership framing
- Do NOT imply the OEM can only own QM/Rust layer — they can extend into ASIL-B SWCs

## Target Audience
- OEM senior management (VP/Director level)
- May include software-defined vehicle program owners, electrical architecture leads
